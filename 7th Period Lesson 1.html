About Brown Bears

The brown bear (Ursus arctos) is native to parts of North America.

It's conservation status is currently Least Concern. There are many subspecies of brown bears

Species

Features

Brown bears are not always brown. Some can be reddish or yellowish. They have very large, curved claws.

Countries with Small Brown Bear Populations

Some countries with smaller brown bear populations include France, Greece and India.
